Why this site?

Too many drills lack structure. Here you get: exactly which drills to perform, how many reps, how to progress, and video demos to copy — all optimized for busy players and coaches.

How to use

  1. Choose a primary skill (Passing, Dribbling, Shooting, Defending, Fitness).
  2. Follow the drill sets in the README and watch the demo videos below.
  3. Record progress and increase difficulty every 1–2 weeks.

Quick Practice Plan (90 minutes)

  • Warm-up — 10–15 min: mobility + ball mastery
  • Technical block — 30 min: passing & dribbling drills
  • Finishing — 20 min: shooting reps under variation
  • Small-sided — 15 min: 4v4 emphasizing the session focus
  • Cool down — 10 min: mobility & recovery
