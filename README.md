## How to use this guide
1. Pick 2–3 skill areas to focus on per session (one primary + one secondary).
2. Warm up 10–15 minutes before drills (dynamic mobility + light ball work).
3. Follow drill sets & progressions below. Finish with conditioning or small-sided games.

## Skill → Drills (what to do, how much, and why)

Note: Warm-up thoroughly before any drill. Rest 60–120s between sets depending on intensity.

1) Passing — Improve accuracy, weight, and decision-making
- Drill: Wall Passing / One-touch passing
  - How: Find a wall or partner, use both feet, alternating passes.
  - Sets: 4 × 2 minutes (one-touch), 3 × 30 passes (two-touch accuracy)
  - Cue: Push through the ball with the inside of the foot; follow-through to target.
  - Progression: Increase distance; add movement after pass (pass + run).
- Drill: Triangle Passing (3 players / cones)
  - How: Short quick passes, one-touch optional.
  - Sets: 6 × 1 minute rotating positions
  - Why: Improves passing under pressure & spatial awareness.

2) Dribbling — Improve close control, change of direction, speed with the ball
- Drill: Cone Weave (tight cones)
  - How: Dribble through cones using inside/outside touches.
  - Sets: 4 × 30s explosive runs, 4 × 30s technical slow focus
  - Cue: Use small touches, head up between cones, plant & push off.
  - Progression: Reduce cone spacing, add a defender.
- Drill: 1v1 Attack Moves
  - Sets: 6 rounds (1 minute each)
  - Why: Improves feints, body shape, beating a defender.

3) Shooting / Finishing — Improve power and placement
- Drill: Finishing from crosses & cutbacks
  - How: Feed balls from wide; finish with driven or placed shot.
  - Sets: 4 × 8–12 shots (vary first-touch, volley)
  - Cue: Strike through the ball, use laces for power, inside for placement.
- Drill: Shooting into target zones (corners)
  - Sets: 3 × 10 shots from different distances.

4) Defending — Improve 1v1, recovery, and team shape
- Drill: 1v1 Defensive Jockey & Tackle
  - How: Defender practices jockeying and timing a tackle.
  - Sets: 6 × 30s reps
  - Cue: Stay balanced, delay attacker, tackle when advantage.
- Drill: Recovery Runs & Defensive Shape (small-sided)
  - Why: Improves positioning and stamina.

5) Fitness & Agility — Soccer-specific conditioning
- Drill: HIIT Shuttle Runs with ball (sprint to cone, control, sprint back)
  - Structure: 6–8 intervals (20–30s) with 60–90s recovery
- Drill: Agility Ladder + Ball mastery
  - Sets: 4 × ladder footwork (fast 20s) then immediate ball control drill

## Mapping drills to improvements (direct answer: which drills to perform)
- Improve Passing Accuracy → Wall passing, Target passing (triangle), One-touch rondos.
- Improve First Touch → Ball mastery drills, short control-and-pass sequences, body-scan touches.
- Improve Dribbling & Close Control → Cone weave, tight-space dribbling, 1v1 moves.
- Improve Finishing → Repetition finishing from various ranges, volley finishing, finishing under pressure.
- Improve Defending → 1v1 defending, recovery runs, small-sided tactical drills.
- Improve Match Fitness → HIIT with ball, shuttle runs, small-sided games.

---

## Sample 6-Week Progression (2 sessions / week focus example)
- Weeks 1–2: Technique & low intensity
  - Session A: Passing (30 min), Dribbling (20 min), Light finishing (10 min)
  - Session B: Ball mastery (20 min), 1v1 drills (25 min), Conditioning (15 min)
- Weeks 3–4: Increase intensity & add pressure
  - Add one-touch rounds, add defenders to dribbling drills, increase shooting speed/pressure.
- Weeks 5–6: Match simulation
  - Small-sided games (4v4/5v5), finishing under fatigue, HIIT conditioning.

## Coaching cues & common mistakes (short)
- Keep head up — scanning is as important as technique.
- Use both feet — spend part of practice forced to your weaker foot.
- Quality over quantity — deliberate repetitions > sloppy high-volume.
- Common mistakes: over-kicking, not setting weight of the pass, poor body position for shooting.

## Example single-session (90 minutes)
- Warm-up (15 min): dynamic + ball mastery
- Technical block (30 min): passing drills 15 + dribbling 15
- Finishing block (20 min): shooting technique & finishing
- Small-sided game (15 min): 4v4 with emphasis (e.g., keep possession)
- Cool down & mobility (10 min)
